FAQ

How to order

All customers are requested to place their order online at Foodland. Add your favorite packages and any additional snacks into the cart, fill in your delivery details and you are done. An automated confirmation email will be sent to your email address after you submit the form, you can pay us via Bank Transfer or by Cash on the first day of delivery.

Please note that you can customize your order by your own on our website. After you add the products into the cart, select Edit instead of Purchase, pick your own dish as your choice and click Save before proceeding to Payment.

You can note down any special request you may have, please limit the number of requests to 2 since too many may confuse our team which can lead to the unwanted misplaced order delivery.

How can i pay

    We offer a variety of payment methods, you can choose your preferred method during checkout.
  • COD: If you choose to pay by cash, please kindly give it to the delivery guy on the first day of delivery. If you are too busy and unable to meet him, please leave an envelope at the reception with a note of your name and the payment amount.
  • Bank Transfer: If you have a Vietnam bank account, please choose Bank Transfer. Currently we offer VCB and ACB account. The Account Info details will be shown after check-out and 1 copy will be sent to your email

Delivery fees and times

  • Delivery fees vary depending on the restaurant and the distance between the restaurant and your delivery address.
  • Delivery times also vary, but most restaurants aim to deliver food within 30-60 minutes of the order being placed.

What if my food is late or arrives cold

  • If your food is late or arrives cold, you should contact the restaurant or the food delivery platform immediately. They may be able to offer you a refund or a discount on your next order.
  • Our process was certified by Food Safety Management Authority of Ho Chi Minh City and highly recommended in the hospitality industry worldwide.
  • We will not deliver frozen or leftover food to you. All of the meals are prepared fresh daily and kept cool in the delivery insolation bag during transportation. Delivering hot/warm food in Saigon's weather can stimulate bacteria growth and make it easier to go stale.
